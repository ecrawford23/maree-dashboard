# MAKING NEW ANGLES FROM ONE RENDER (reframe technique)
Nano Banana can re-frame a single render into several shots. After (or instead of) a straight conversion, add ONE of these to the prompt — keep the building identical:
- **Low hero angle:** "…from a low street-level angle looking up at the building, dramatic but realistic perspective."
- **Tight crop / detail:** "…a tight photographic crop on the entrance and signage, shallow depth of field."
- **Wider context:** "…a wider establishing frame showing more of the street and sky."
- **Telephoto compression:** "…shot on an 85mm lens, compressed perspective, strong background blur."
- **Eye-level pedestrian POV:** "…eye-level as if a person standing on the sidewalk is taking the photo."
> Generate 2–3 per render; you'll build a deep library fast. (Caution: bigger reframes = more invention, so compare to the original and keep the ones that stay true.)
