## ⭐ THE BASE PROMPT — paste this first on EVERY shot
> Turn this 3D architectural rendering into a real photograph. It should look like a real photo taken on a Sony A7R V with a 35mm f/4 lens, ISO 100 — not a render, not CGI. Keep the exact buildings, architecture, balconies, storefronts, signage, layout, and camera angle unchanged. Replace every 3D figure with a real photographed person — varied ages and body types, candid mid-stride poses, individual wrinkled clothing, sharp realistic detail. Replace rendered cars with real cars with authentic paint, accurate reflections, and faint road grime. Real, imperfect materials: stucco with faint mottling, brick with mortar and stain variation, glass with true reflections and minor smudges, wet asphalt with realistic specular reflections, brick pavers with subtle wear. Real foliage — palms with individual irregular fronds. Shallow depth of field with the far background slightly soft, fine natural film grain, faint lens vignette, subtle atmospheric haze. True-to-life color, not over-saturated. Photorealistic and indistinguishable from a real DSLR photo — no plastic textures, no waxy skin, no perfect symmetry, no extra text or watermarks.

**Then add the one scene line below.** Then run ONE refinement: *"sharpen the people into real photographed humans and add subtle depth of field — keep everything else identical."* Don't push past that or the building drifts.
