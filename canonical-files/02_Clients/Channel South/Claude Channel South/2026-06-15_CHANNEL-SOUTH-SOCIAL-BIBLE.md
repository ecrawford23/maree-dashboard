## 1 · THE REAL STORY (verified facts — your content fuel)
- **$105M mixed-use waterfront district**, downtown Gulfport, 4.34 acres at **25th Ave & West Beach Blvd**.
- Led by **AnderCorp, LLC + Bellamare Development**. Mayor **Hugh Keating** publicly behind it.
- Built on the **historic Great Southern Hotel site** (1903 landmark).
- **Phase 1:** 136 luxury residences · ~17,637 sq ft retail/dining · **Hotel Holden** (114-room Marriott Tribute) · 294-space garage · pedestrian streetscapes + public space.
- **Timeline:** construction began **Dec 2025** → parking **2026** → residences + hotel through **2027**. *(Not an imminent open — frame as "the rise," milestones, heritage. No "weeks to doors.")*
- **Hotel Holden:** rooftop over **Jones Park and the Harbor**; "coastal legacy," "rooted in story, atmosphere, experience."
- Jobs: ~125–175 construction, ~75 hospitality.

**Heritage fact bank** (for decks/Reels/video): Great Southern Hotel — 250 rooms, telephone in every room, hot/cold water, electric chandeliers, own well + power plant (1903); demolished 1951. Donald Ross golf course (1908), oldest in MS, closed 2022. **Capt. Joseph T. Jones** — oil baron, co-founded Gulfport, built the railroad, dredged the channel that made the Port of Gulfport; paid Capt. Avegno $1,000 to prove it (Jan 24, 1902); "city's greatest benefactor." **Jones Park** donated 1935 by Grace Jones Stewart. Maritime naming sources (internal, names NOT public): shipwrights, ship chandlers, carrick bend, Sandhill crane, oleander, longleaf pine.

**Recipe:** palette = deep coastal teal/slate + Hotel Holden dusty blue + cream/sand + brass-navy. Type = elegant serif + script. **Color-grade every render, photo, and graphic to one warm, sunlit, calm tone** (biggest fast visual win). Sell the feeling/the day, not the drywall.

## 10 · INTERACTIVE SYSTEM + STORY SCRIPTS
**The test:** every interactive piece must (1) move toward a follow/lead, (2) give data we use, or (3) build narrative/community. No vanity taps.

**The 6 jobs:** 1) convert viewers→followers · 2) capture leasing leads · 3) earn reach via signals · 4) learn the audience · 5) community + narrative control · 6) UGC at events.

### FRAME-BY-FRAME STORY SCRIPTS

**JOB 1 · "Follow the rise" teaser** *(run on reveal days — #3, #15)*
- **Frame 1:** blurred/zoomed Hotel Holden render. Text: *"Something's coming to the Gulfport waterfront…"*
- **Frame 2:** partial reveal. Text: *"A 114-room rooftop hotel over Jones Park."* Sticker: **quiz** "Guess what it is?" (Hotel / Apartments / Restaurant).
- **Frame 3:** full render. Text: *"Meet Hotel Holden."* Sticker: **"Follow so you don't miss the next reveal"** + follow nudge.
- **Loop:** check Net Follows that day; if it spikes, lock one reveal-teaser/week.

**JOB 2 · Leasing lead-capture** *(run Fridays / on residence + retail posts)*
- **Frame 1:** residence or streetscape render. Text: *"Want to be first in line at Channel South?"*
- **Frame 2:** Text: *"136 waterfront residences + curated retail, coming to downtown Gulfport."* Sticker: **question box** — *"Drop your email and we'll keep you posted on leasing."*
- **Frame 3:** Text: *"Or reach us anytime."* Sticker: **link** → channelsouth.com.
- **Loop:** paste every response into a **Leads tab/sheet**. This is the pipeline metric that matters most.

**JOB 3 · Reach signals (attached to a real post only):**
- *Guess-the-year* (with C3): Frame — "A telephone in every room… guess the year." **Poll/slider** → next frame reveal *1903*.
- *This-or-That* (with #4): "Rooftop sunset or harbor morning?" **poll**.
- *Quiz* (with C4): "Who founded Gulfport?" **quiz sticker** → reveal Capt. Jones.

**JOB 4 · Audience research (low weeks):** question box *"What do you most want on the Gulfport waterfront — coffee, rooftop, retail, a restaurant?"* + poll *"Local to the Coast, or watching from away?"* → **log answers** for insight.

**JOB 5 · Community / narrative:** *"Do you remember the Great Southern Hotel? Share your photos — we'll repost."* + on civic posts, a hopes-for-downtown box → reply in the **positive economic-engine voice**; log recurring concerns.

**JOB 6 · UGC at events (near open):** signature **rooftop photo moment** + a branded hashtag; reshare tagged content.

**Weekly Stories rhythm:** Mon reshare + follow nudge · Tue post's poll/quiz · Wed research/community box · Thu Holden tap-to-reveal · Fri leasing link. 3–5 frames/day, always tied to a real post/question.
