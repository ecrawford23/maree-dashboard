## FORMATS
- **Carousels** (depth + saves) — heritage series, Meet the District.
- **Reels** (reach/discovery — your growth lever) — render fly-throughs, watch-it-rise, rooftop mood. Cut from the 4K flythrough + photoreal stills.
- **Static** (proof + brand graphics) — milestones, quotes, reveals.
- **Stories** (the engine) — 6 jobs: follow conversion, **lead capture**, reach signals, audience research, community, UGC.

## THE LOOK (locked direction)
Editorial, boutique-hotel, **image-led** — real photo full-bleed, elegant **serif + script**, warm filmic grade, the **buoy + EST·1903** lockup, lots of breathing room. Palette: teal/navy · dusty blue · stone/sand · cream · brass. *No flat text-cards, no "ad" feeling.* Built custom in Canva over **real archival + render imagery**, not AI auto-layouts.

## THE PIPELINE (how a post gets made)
1. **Plan** — it's in the calendar (pillar, format, hook, date/time).
2. **Words** — caption + carousel copy already drafted (Bible).
3. **Image** — pull the real render/archival; color-grade warm.
4. **Design** — drop into the locked Canva template (cover + interior + CTA).
5. **Schedule** — Meta Business Suite, midday CT.
6. **Engage** — Stories + reply in-voice; capture leads.
7. **Measure** — Friday, into the Analytics tab.
