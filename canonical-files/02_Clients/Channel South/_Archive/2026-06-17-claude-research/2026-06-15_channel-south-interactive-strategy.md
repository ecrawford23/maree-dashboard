# Channel South — Interactive Content Strategy
*Evie · 2026-06-15 · Intentional interactive posts + Stories. Every unit below has a JOB and a closed loop — what we DO with the response. If a piece doesn't pass the test, it doesn't run.*

> **The test (so it's never content-for-content's-sake):** every interactive piece must do at least one of —
> **(1)** move someone toward a *follow* or a *lead*, **(2)** give us *data we actually use*, or **(3)** genuinely *build the narrative / community.* If it only earns a vanity tap, cut it.

> **Why interactive matters *for this account specifically* (from your data):** taps, replies, votes, and DMs are the strongest signals the algorithm rewards — and your discovery engine is already working (57–76% of views are non-followers). Interactive turns those passing viewers into engaged ones, which is exactly the leak we need to close on IG. So interactive isn't decoration here; it's the conversion mechanism.

---

## THE 6 JOBS (each interactive unit maps to one)

### JOB 1 · Convert viewers → followers  *(fixes the IG leak: 783 visits, ~0 follows)*
**Unit: "Follow the rise" Story + reveal teaser.** A blurred render → "Tap to watch it rise. Follow so you don't miss the reveal" + a **follow reminder / "Add yours" reaction**.
- **Loop:** track Net Follows in Insights the day it runs. If reveals spike follows (they do — Hotel Holden was your biggest day), schedule one reveal-teaser per week.

### JOB 2 · Capture leasing leads  *(inbound is currently ZERO despite real interest)*
**Unit: "Want in?" intent sticker.** On residence/retail posts → Story with a **question sticker**: "Curious about leasing at Channel South? Drop your email and we'll keep you posted." + a **link sticker** to channelsouth.com.
- **Loop:** every response goes into a **leads list** (a simple sheet/tab). This is the one that turns social into pipeline — it's the highest-value interactive you have.

### JOB 3 · Earn reach via engagement signals  *(feed the discovery engine)*
**Unit: "Guess the year / This-or-That" polls + quizzes** on the heritage decks.
- "A telephone in every room — guess the year." → reveal **1903**.
- "Rooftop sunset or harbor morning?" (this-or-that).
- **Loop:** these are low-lift and algorithm-friendly — but they only run *attached to a real post* (the fun-fact carousel, the rooftop reveal), never standalone. The job is to extend a post that already matters, not manufacture filler.

### JOB 4 · Learn the audience  *(we have zero demographics + no comment data yet)*
**Unit: research questions disguised as engagement.**
- Question box: "What do you most want on the Gulfport waterfront — coffee, rooftop, retail, a restaurant?"
- Poll: "Are you local to the Coast, or watching from away?"
- **Loop:** tally the answers → this becomes real audience insight (who's watching, what they want) that informs tenant talking points *and* future content. We're using free engagement to do market research.

### JOB 5 · Build community + control the narrative  *(there's a backlash history here)*
**Unit: "Share your memory" UGC + proactive positive framing.**
- "Do you remember the Great Southern Hotel? Share your photos and memories — we'll repost." (nostalgia + UGC)
- On civic/economic posts, a question box inviting hopes for downtown → respond in the **positive economic-engine voice** (per the team's playbook).
- **Loop:** repost the best memories (free, warm content); log recurring concerns so we can address them proactively — turning comments into narrative control, not just chatter.

### JOB 6 · Drive UGC + reach at the events  *(once activations/open begin)*
**Unit: a signature hashtag + photo moment.** Claim the **rooftop over Jones Park** as the icon (your Wharf-OB-style "Instagrammable moment") + a branded hashtag for reposting.
- **Loop:** reshare tagged content; the hashtag aggregates UGC into a free, ongoing feed.

## WEEKLY STORIES RHYTHM (light, repeatable — not a grind)
- **Mon:** reshare the day's feed post to Stories + a follow reminder. *(Job 1)*
- **Tue:** the interactive tied to that day's carousel — poll or quiz. *(Job 3)*
- **Wed:** one research/community prompt — question box or this-or-that. *(Jobs 4–5)*
- **Thu:** Hotel Holden "tap to reveal" teaser. *(Job 1)*
- **Fri:** leasing/link sticker on a render + "DM us." *(Job 2)*
3–5 frames a day max. Always tied to a real post or a real question — never filler.

---

## WHAT WE MEASURE (so we know it's working, not just busy)
- **Job 1:** Net follows on reveal/teaser days.
- **Job 2:** # of leasing replies/emails captured (the number that matters most).
- **Job 3:** poll/quiz response counts + whether reach lifts on those posts.
- **Job 4:** what the audience actually says (logged for insight).
- **Job 5:** memories submitted + sentiment of responses.
Review these Friday in the Analytics & Goals tab. Drop anything that isn't doing its job.

---

### Bottom line
This is built so each interactive piece is pulling a lever you actually need — **follows, leads, reach, insight, or narrative** — and every one closes its loop. The two that matter most for Channel South right now: **Job 1 (fix the follow leak)** and **Job 2 (capture leasing leads)**. Those aren't engagement bait; they're the conversion + pipeline this account is currently missing.
