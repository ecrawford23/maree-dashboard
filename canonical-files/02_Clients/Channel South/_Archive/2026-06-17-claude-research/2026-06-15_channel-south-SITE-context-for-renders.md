## Where it is
Downtown Gulfport, MS. One block:
- **South edge:** Beach Boulevard (US-90) — the waterfront road.
- **North edge:** 13th Street.
- **West:** 27th Avenue · **East:** 25th Avenue.
- **North is up; the Gulf/water is to the SOUTH.** Across Beach Blvd south = **Jones Park**, the **Gulfport Municipal Marina**, the beach, the Mississippi Sound. (Port of Gulfport SW; Mississippi Aquarium E.)

## The program (WBA labeled key) — 136 residences total
- **Hotel (Hotel Holden)** — central/south block fronting Beach Boulevard. **Hotel Motor Plaza + Hotel Entrance off 25th Avenue (east) side**, hotel amenity, and the **Rooftop Bar on top facing south over Beach Blvd → Jones Park + the marina.**
- **Residences — 136 units total:**
  - 4-floor multifamily, **80 units**, parking on ground floor — the big L/U-shaped building on the **west/south** wrapping the courtyard.
  - Mixed-use, 4-floor multifamily **36 units**, with **8,500 sf retail/restaurant on the ground floor** — **north, along 13th Street.**
  - **Studio / Jr. 1-bed living, 20 units** — northeast (13th St / 25th Ave corner).
- **Pool / Clubhouse + amenity areas** — interior **courtyard** of the 80-unit building (southwest).
- **Parking deck: 288 spaces** — central core; plus surface parking NW and along 27th Avenue.
- **Existing historic office building** — retained, north-central on 13th Street (a real heritage anchor on site).

## What this means for prompts (intentional, not generic)
- **Sunlight:** golden-hour sun sets in the **WEST** (over 27th Ave / the Port). Use *"low warm sun from the west, long shadows pointing east."*
- **Water + views:** the **rooftop bar and any water-facing shot look SOUTH** over Beach Blvd → Jones Park, the marina, and the lighthouse — matches the Hotel Holden rooftop render exactly.
- **Street life + retail:** energy is along **Beach Boulevard (south face)** and the **25th Avenue corner** (hotel entrance + mixed-use retail). Pedestrian retail also on 13th St (mixed-use ground floor).
- **The heritage beat:** there's a **real retained historic office building on 13th Street** — plus the Great Southern Hotel legacy. "Built where the coast began" is literal: the site faces the historic waterfront, marina, and Gulf.
