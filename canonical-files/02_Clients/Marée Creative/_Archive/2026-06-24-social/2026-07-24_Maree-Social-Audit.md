## Voice + Execution Guardrails

**Every post must:**
1. **Show, don't tell** — "We've built [specific project] that achieved [specific outcome]" vs. "We're great at branding"
2. **Lead with proof** — What we've actually done, not what we claim
3. **Be specific** — Name the place. Name the number. Name the outcome. Vague = cheap.
4. **Use restraint** — One strong idea per post. Trust white space. No buzzwords.
5. **Sound editorial** — Warm, confident, like a trusted partner; never corporate
6. **End with a clear CTA** — Save this, share this, follow for more, comment with your experience

**Voice breakers (never do):**
- Buzzwords: "best-in-class," "results-driven," "innovative," "synergy," "leverage," "think outside the box"
- Stacked adjectives: "beautifully designed, strategically positioned, award-winning..."
- Hype: Superlatives, all-caps energy, fake urgency
- Vague passion: "We're excited to announce..." / "Thrilled to share..."
- Promotional copy: Deliverables talk. Outcome talk.
