## Executive Summary

**The Competitive Landscape:**
Most creative agencies in the development/luxury real estate space are playing the same game: finished-work Instagram portfolios and LinkedIn thought leadership. It's a crowded, undifferentiated space where everyone talks about brand strategy but few show *how* they actually do it.

**Marée's Competitive Advantage:**
You're embedded inside a development company. That single fact changes *everything*. Your competitors are consultants; you're practitioners. They claim; you show. This should be your primary differentiator across both Instagram and especially TikTok.

**The Opportunity:**
1. **TikTok is uncontested** — None of your direct competitors are on TikTok. You can own the "brand strategy education" space there.
2. **Process is unshown** — Everyone shows finished work; nobody shows the strategy sessions, naming sketches, or interior detail iterations that get you there.
3. **Real estate metrics are untold** — Competitors talk about "brand impact"; you can talk about leasing velocity, capital movement, occupancy rate.
4. **Team is invisible** — Agencies hide behind the logo; you can lead with the people who've built what they brand.

**July Strategy:** Position Marée as the "proof-driven" alternative to hype-heavy agencies. Show the process, the metrics, the team. Use TikTok to own the educational space competitors haven't touched.

## What's Working for Competitors

### Streetsense
**Format: Behind-the-Scenes Montage**
- Quick cuts of design process, white-boarding, team collaboration
- 500–2K likes per post (strong engagement for their follower count)
- **Why it works:** Shows the work happening; reveals the thinking; feels authentic vs. polish-heavy
- **Marée application:** You can out-do this by showing *real* development meetings, naming workshops, interior decisions in a development context

### Green Olive
**Format: Interior Detail Series + Process Carousel**
- Close-up shots of materials, lighting, finishes paired with mood boards
- 400–1.5K likes per post
- **Why it works:** Luxe aesthetic + strategic thinking paired together; proves design is intentional
- **Marée application:** You have access to finished Bellamare projects + interior photography that competitors can't match; emphasize the material choices as *brand decisions*

### Pentagram
**Format: Minimalist Portfolio Showcase**
- Beautiful, sparse feeds with high-quality photography + minimal text
- 2–5K likes per post (scale advantage)
- **Why it works:** Authority through minimalism; finished work speaks for itself
- **Marée application:** You can adopt this *look* for Instagram (clean, minimal, specific) while Pentagram has no process/educational content — that's your gap to own

## What's NOT Working (Gaps)

### Across All Competitors:
1. **No process education** — They show finished work; none explain *how* they got there
2. **No real estate metrics talk** — They discuss "brand impact"; none quantify leasing velocity, occupancy, capital
3. **Rare team visibility** — They hide behind the work; almost no "meet the strategist" content
4. **Zero TikTok presence** — All are Instagram/LinkedIn only (massive opportunity)
5. **No philosophy content** — Nobody talks about *why* they approach brand differently
6. **Infrequent posting** — 2–3x/week means algorithm doesn't favor them; 4–5x/week is where growth compounds

### LinkedIn-only Weakness:
All competitors use LinkedIn for thought leadership but **only because Instagram is "too visual"** for strategy talk. This is false. TikTok proves educational content about brand strategy can thrive short-form, and Marée can be the first in your space to do it.

#### 1. **TikTok Educational Authority**
**The Gap:** Zero competitors on TikTok. All brand strategy education happens on LinkedIn (boring) or nowhere.

- Educational quick-cuts that competitors will copy in 6–12 months

**Timeline:** Weeks 1–4 of July. Post 4–5 TikToks first, before Instagram.

**Why Marée Wins:** You're embedded inside development; you understand real estate metrics in a way consultants don't. Your TikTok content can be "developer-intelligent brand strategy" — the intersection nobody's talking about.

#### 2. **Real Estate Metrics Talk**
**The Gap:** Every competitor talks about "brand impact" vaguely. None discuss leasing velocity, capital movement, occupancy rates.

**How to Own It:**
- "The metrics that actually matter in brand strategy" (occupancy, leasing velocity, revenue per sqft)

**Why Marée Wins:** You're NOT an outside agency giving advice; you've been inside on real estate projects. You know what move the needle. This credibility is nuclear.

#### 3. **Process + Transparency (Full Journey)**
**The Gap:** Streetsense shows process; Green Olive shows interiors; neither shows both + the strategy that connects them.

**How to Own It:**

- Instagram carousel: discovery notes → naming sketches → interior renderings → finished photography
- TikTok: Quick cuts of each phase with voiceover explaining the why
- Behind-the-scenes video of an actual client workshop (with permission)

**Why Marée Wins:** You have access to Bellamare's project timeline + interior photography that external agencies don't. Show the full journey from strategy to space.

## Recommended Competitive Positioning

**Positioning Statement:**  
"Marée is the creative studio for real estate that's been inside the business. We don't advise from the outside; we've built what we brand. We speak in metrics (leasing, occupancy, capital) that matter to developers. Our brand work is tested, transparent, and outcome-focused."

**How to Own This:**
- **Content:** Show process + metrics (not just finished work)
- **Tone:** Specific + proof-led (not vague + hype)
- **Platforms:** Lead on TikTok (where competitors aren't); own Instagram with education (where they only show portfolios)
- **Team:** Make the people visible (competitors hide behind logos)

**What Competitors Can't Copy:**
- Being embedded inside a development company (structural advantage)
- Speaking developer language (leasing velocity, capital, pro forma implications)
- Access to finished Bellamare projects + interior photography (assets advantage)
- Your specific voice (warm + developer-intelligent + no hype)
