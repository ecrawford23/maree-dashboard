## POSITIONING & VOICE FOR GALLERY MADISON SOCIAL

**The Brand Feeling:**
*"The brand is a feeling before it's a place."* — Gallery Madison Brand Brief

Gallery Madison isn't a mall. It's not a development. It's a **third place**—where a 7-year-old builds memories and a 45-year-old finds community on the same evening, no apologies.

**Voice (applying Marée discipline):**
- **Specific over vague.** Name Spinners (120k sq ft). Name Royal Street. Name the local tenants as they announce. "Built by Madison, for Madison" isn't a slogan—it's the story we prove every post.
- **Proof-first.** Show the groundbreaking. Show the site. Show the blueprints. No hype—let the scale and the vision speak.
- **One strong idea per post.** Don't pile. A single image of the lot with "Foundation rising. July." beats three carousel images of half-formed phases.
- **White space.** Let renders breathe. Captions are short. Restraint builds anticipation.
- **Community as hero.** When tenants announce, celebrate the *person* who will run it, not the tenant logo. When construction crews work, they're *Madisonians* building Madison.

**Tone:** Editorial, warm, developer-intelligent. Like reading a trade journal written by someone who actually built it.

## CONTENT STRATEGY: IG vs. FACEBOOK

### **INSTAGRAM** (Primary discovery engine)
- **Audience:** Younger, visual-first, decision-makers (25–45), local design + architecture interest.
- **Frequency:** 3–4×/week (1 planned + 2 spontaneous/site moments).
- **Format:** Single image carousel (2–3 slides max), Reels for time-lapse/site, Stories for behind-the-scenes.
- **Hook:** One concrete detail. Spinners' 120k sq ft. Royal Street's French-inspired street. The 52 concert nights/year. Make them want to know more.
- **Bio:** "Madison's new third place. Spinners. Royal Street. Where everyone gathers. Opens Nov '26. #DreamBigMadison"

**Why this approach:**
- IG users scroll for discovery—we need one stopping detail (Spinners is that hook).
- Reels performance will drive awareness; use construction time-lapse as anchor content.
- Strong architecture + family experience positioning will perform well with Madison's design-conscious + young-family audience.

### **FACEBOOK** (Community + trust engine)
- **Audience:** Families (35–55), civic/community groups, local business owners, season ticketholders.
- **Frequency:** 2×/week (longer-form, educational).
- **Format:** Single image + longer caption, video, or album (5–8 images for construction progression).
- **Hook:** Community impact. Job creation. Local business wins. The Friday night experience coming.
- **Bio:** "The Gallery Madison—Arts & Entertainment District. Built by Madison, for Madison. Openings start November 2026."

**Why this approach:**
- FB users want context and narrative—we give them "here's what it means for Madison."
- Albums work well for before/after construction; gives families a way to track progress.
- Longer captions allow us to name local partners, tenants, and impact stories—proof of community integration.
- Community messaging ("Built by Madison, for Madison") resonates stronger on FB than IG.

## COMPETITOR AUDIT INSIGHTS (Updated from GALLERY-COMPETITOR-AUDIT.md)

**Key Differentiation Opportunities** (What Competitors Miss):

Gallery Madison has six major competitive advantages. Rivals focus on retail announcements and event programming. They miss:

1. **Founder/Developer Origin Story** (All 5 competitors silent)
   - **Gallery Madison owns:** Developer spotlights, design philosophy, vision storytelling
   - **Why:** Humanizes the project; zero competitors doing this
   - **Action:** Weekly founder posts; "Why We Built This" narrative series

2. **Job Creation & Economic Impact Messaging** (All 5 competitors silent)
   - **Gallery Madison owns:** "Hiring 150 local workers" announcement; employee spotlights with names/roles; construction job documentation
   - **Why:** Civic goodwill + media appeal; competitors ignore this entirely
   - **Action:** Weekly job/hiring announcements; employee spotlight Reels; quarterly impact reports

3. **Tenant Founder Story Deep-Dives** (Halcyon does minimally; others ignore)
   - **Gallery Madison owns:** "Meet the Team" video series; "Why Spinners Chose Gallery Madison" content; chef/operator spotlights
   - **Why:** Builds community trust; reciprocal promotion multiplier effect
   - **Action:** 3–5 tenant founder interview carousel posts; Reel spotlights with "Why I'm Here" angle

4. **Reel Dominance** (All competitors underutilize)
   - **Gallery Madison target:** 60–70% Reels vs. competitors' 20–30% usage
   - **Why:** 36% reach advantage; strongest new follower acquisition in 2026
   - **Action:** Weekly construction time-lapses, countdown Reels, tenant spotlights, employee interviews all in Reel format

5. **Rapid Community Response** (No competitor demonstrates this)
   - **Gallery Madison commits:** <1 hour DM/comment response time
   - **Why:** 2026 algorithmic advantage; builds early community loyalty
   - **Action:** Dedicated community manager day 1; response protocol; daily Q&A Stories

6. **Cross-Promotion Strategy with Tenants** (Competitors announce, don't amplify)
   - **Gallery Madison approach:** "Tenant Spotlight Fridays"—feature one tenant weekly with sharable graphics; ask tenants to repost
   - **Why:** 5–10x reach multiplier (tenant followers × Gallery followers)
   - **Action:** Formal tenant cross-promotion plan; weekly graphic packs; reciprocal repost commitments

**Format Mix (Optimized for 2026):**
- **60–70% Reels** (construction time-lapses, countdowns, tenant spotlights, crew interviews, opening day energy)
- **20–30% Carousels** (architectural/design stories, before-and-after, tenant profiles, job creation highlights)
- **10% Static Posts** (milestone announcements, impact reports, community partnerships)
- **Daily Stories** (behind-the-scenes, crew spotlights, countdown, Q&A)

## VOICE CHECKLIST (For Every Post)

Before posting, does it pass Marée voice discipline?

- [ ] **Specific, not vague.** Did I name the place, the number, the outcome? *(Not: "amazing construction." Yes: "120,000 sq ft of Spinners rising.")*
- [ ] **One idea.** Is there ONE strong hook, or did I pile three ideas?
- [ ] **Proof-first.** Am I showing work or making claims? *(Shows: site photo. Claims: "most family-friendly district")*
- [ ] **Community as hero.** Am I celebrating Madisonians, or celebrating Bellamare? *(Yes: "Meet the Madison contractors building..." No: "Bellamare is proud to announce...")*
- [ ] **White space.** Is the caption short? Does the image breathe? *(Yes: "Foundation rising." No: "Our pioneering mixed-use entertainment destination featuring world-class amenities and thoughtfully curated tenant experiences...")*
- [ ] **No buzzwords.** Did I avoid "world-class," "results-driven," "best-in-class," "game-changer"? ✓
