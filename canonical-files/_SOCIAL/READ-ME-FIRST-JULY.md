## 📋 Voice Checklist (Before You Post)

Every piece of content should pass **all 5 of these:**

- [ ] **Specific** — Named the place? Named the number? Named the outcome? (Avoid vague language)
- [ ] **Proof-led** — Leading with what we've built, not what we claim?
- [ ] **Warm + editorial** — Sounds like a trusted partner, not a vendor?
- [ ] **Developer-intelligent** — Using real estate / business language, not creative hype?
- [ ] **Restrained** — One strong idea? Trusting white space? No buzzwords?

**If you check all 5:** Post it.  
**If you check 3–4:** Edit and try again.  
**If you check fewer than 3:** Scrap it and start over.

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Avoid These)

1. **Posting polished corporate video on TikTok** — TikTok rewards authentic B-roll + voiceover, not highly produced corporate video. Ship it.
2. **Vague captions** — "We create memorable experiences" ❌ → "Channel South went from 40% to 89% occupancy in 18 months" ✅
3. **Buzzwords** — "Best-in-class," "results-driven," "innovative," "synergy" — these kill credibility.
4. **Inconsistent posting** — One perfect post per month loses to mediocre posts 4x/week. Consistency beats perfection.
5. **Forgetting the CTA** — Every post needs a specific ask: Save, Comment, Follow, Visit bio link, DM.
