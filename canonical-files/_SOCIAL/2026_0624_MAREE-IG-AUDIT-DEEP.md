## 2. BIO AUDIT

**Current bio (full text):**
> We build the brand + execute the vision.
> Hospitality · Districts · Retail
> Built inside @bellamaredevelopment
> ↓ Start...

**What's working:** Short, declarative first line. Sector dots are clean. The Bellamare tag is smart — developer credibility in one line.

**What's not:**

First line — "We build the brand + execute the vision" — is generic enough that any agency could claim it. It doesn't carry Marée's specific competitive edge: that the people doing the branding have closed the land, run the pro forma, and opened the doors.

The official tagline — **"Our work begins where other creative agencies end"** — does not appear anywhere on the profile. That's the sharpest line Marée has and it's sitting unused.

"↓ Start" truncates into nothing, which is fine if it expands on mobile, but visually it reads unfinished.

**Recommended bio rewrite:**
> Our work begins where other creative agencies end.
> Brand strategy + creative direction — built inside @bellamaredevelopment
> Hospitality · Districts · Retail · Placemaking
> ↓ [Link text]

## 11. WHAT'S WORKING

- **Cross-tagging client accounts** — the only posts that broke through engagement-wise (Post 7: 11 likes, Post 8: 7 likes) both reference @thegallerymadison. The model works.
- **Editorial caption structure** — Posts 6 and 8 prove the brand voice lands when executed correctly. Short declarative lines, sensory specifics, developer framing.
- **Reel format** — Post 5 Reel (6 likes) outperformed most static posts despite the account being tiny. Reels reach beyond the existing follower base.
- **Visual aesthetic direction** — the mood board and architectural photography direction is consistent with the brand's positioning (restraint, editorial, developer-credible).
- **The Bellamare connection** — tagging @bellamaredevelopment and naming the parent company is a smart credibility shortcut at this stage.

---

## 12. WHAT'S MISSING

In order of priority:

1. **Posting consistency** — 12-day gap since June 12. Content can't build a following if it disappears.
2. **The official tagline** — "Our work begins where other creative agencies end" appears nowhere on the profile.
3. **Highlights** — the profile has no Highlights. Zero.
4. **Hashtags on every post** — 8 of 10 posts have none.
5. **Carousels** — zero in 10 posts. Carousels drive saves (the highest-value engagement signal) and let a single idea breathe across multiple frames.
6. **Specific proof in captions** — project names, square footage, opening dates, outcomes. The brand guide says to name the place and the number. No post does this yet.
7. **Cross-promotion** — @channelsouthms and @germantownvillage haven't been cross-tagged once.
8. **A CTAs strategy** — most posts end with no direction. "Save this" or "follow for more" aren't corporate — they're how the algorithm understands that content has value.
9. **Owned project imagery** — all 10 posts use stock, borrowed, or atmosphere photography. As Marée's own project work becomes photographable (Channel South, Gallery), this should shift to owned.
10. **A pinned post that actually converts** — the current pin is the weakest caption in the feed.
