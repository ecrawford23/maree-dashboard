# 📱 _SOCIAL — your one home for everything social
*Every account looks the same. You never have to decide where something goes.*

## The whole rule (that's it)
Open the account → drop the file in the numbered folder that matches where it is:

1. **1_Assets** — raw stuff to pull from: photos, renders, video clips, logos
2. **2_Drafts** — posts you're still building
3. **3_Ready to Post** — finished, just needs scheduling
4. **4_Posted** — drag it here once it's live (your archive)

A post just moves **1 → 2 → 3 → 4** as it gets done. The numbers are the order.

## The two things, and what each is for
- **This folder (`_SOCIAL`)** = where the **content lives** (the files).
- **`Maree_Social_Command_Center.xlsx`** (right here in this folder) = **what to do & when** (the tracker + dashboard).

Files here. Plan there. That's the system.
